# Design: Professional Calendar View

## Concept
A clean, productivity-focused monthly calendar. No neon, no holograms. Just a functional, highly readable full-month view.

## Visuals
*   **The Container:** A clean white card with soft shadows.
*   **The Grids:** Standard 7-column calendar layout (Sun - Sat).
*   **The Days:**
    *   **Present:** Soft Emerald background with dark text.
    *   **Rejected:** Soft Red background.
    *   **Pending:** Soft Amber outline.
    *   **Absent/Weekend:** Clear/White.
*   **Typography:** Clean Inter/San-serif.

## Interaction
*   **Selection:** Tapping a day highlights it.
*   **Detail Panel:** A simple section below the calendar shows the specific details for the selected day (Time, Location, Notes).
